Bosses
There are seven pre-hardmode bosses and ten hardmode bosses. Here are all the bosses from weakest to strongest.
    Pre-hardmode:
  1. King Slime
  2. Eye of Cthulhu
  3. Eater of worlds/brain of Cthulhu
  4. Queen bee
  5. Skelletron
  6. Wall of Flesh
    Hardmode:
  1. Queen Slime
  2. Destroyer
  3. The Twins
  4. Skeletron prime
  5. Plantera
  6. Golem
  7. Empress of light
  8. Duke Fishron
  9. Lunatic Cultist
  10. Moon Lord
